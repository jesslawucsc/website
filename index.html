Jess Law 海琪

hɔɪ keɪ  ·  she / her

I study how meaning is structured, interpreted, and communicated — with a particular focus on semantics, pragmatics, and experimental approaches to language. I'm a faculty member in the Department of Linguistics at the University of California, Santa Cruz.
